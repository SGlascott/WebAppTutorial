The Temple University bulletin called TUHappening will allow students to publicly or anonymously discuss topics about everything Temple. Students can talk about classes, news, sports, clubs, faculty, services, or anything else they might want to discuss. It is an open forum to Temple Students only! Students can create new topics to talk about or find other topics to talk to students about. Clubs on campus can advertise events, students can discuss classes or the professors that run them, chat about Rate My Professor rankings, or comment on local eats and bars! Sign up today to see what your peers think about the community around you.
